Lasagna

Description

Chicken lasagna with spinach and a creamy white cheese sauce. Great for any kind of potluck. Kids love it!

Ingredients

  • Noodles 200 gm
  • Butter: to cook onion and garlic
  • Vegetables: 1 Onion and 2 packs of Spinach
  • Garlic
  • All Purpose Flour: To thicken the sauce
  • Broth (Chicken)
  • Milk
  • Seasoning and herbs (salt, dried basil, dried oregano, black pepper, and fresh parsley)
  • Cheese
  • Chicken

    • Steps

    1. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles.
    2. Cook the onion and garlic in butter, then whisk in the flour. Add the broth, milk, and salt.
    3. Add some of the mozzarella and Parmesan. Season with basil, oregano, and pepper. Set aside.
    4. Assemble the lasagna according to the recipe.
    5. Bake in the preheated oven.
