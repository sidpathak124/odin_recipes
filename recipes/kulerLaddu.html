Lasagna

Description

A nutritious sweet, eaten in lots of festivals and can be cooked very quickly.

Ingredients

  • Pearl millet flour
  • Jaggery
  • Ghee

    • Steps

    1. Take bajra flour and jaggery in a bowl.
    2. Rub them with your fingers to break jaggery lumps and mix both really well. It will take good 5-7 minutes.
    3. Melt the ghee in a small saucepan or in the microwave. Ghee should be warm to touch (not hot). Add into flour-jaggery mixture.
